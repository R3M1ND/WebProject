Raven Tail
Raven Tail is a powerful Guild that was founded by Makarov's son,Ivan.
It was formerly a Dark Guild pre-timeskip but was approved by the Magic-

Raven Tail participated in the Grand Magic Games of X791 and placed 3rd
in the preliminaries.
Ivan Dreyar
Phantom Lord
Once, Phantom Lord was the most powerful Magic guild in Fiore.
The guild master, Jose Porla, one of the Ten Wizard Saints, then instigated
a guild war with Fairy Tail out of jealousy. After Phantom Lord was defeated,
the Magic Council dissolved the guild and stripped Jose of his positions.
